About the fundraiser

Your donation provides menstrual products to 300+ young girls in government schools and colleges, helping end period poverty and providing dignity and health.

Who we are

Registered under the Indian Trusts Act and listed on NITI Aayog’s NGO-DARPAN.

At Code Crimson, we believe that menstrual health is not a privilege — it’s a basic right. We are a non-profit organization dedicated to breaking taboos, spreading awareness, and empowering communities through menstrual education.

Through our initiatives, we’ve distributed 800+ reusable sanitary pads to those in need, providing a sustainable and eco-friendly solution while promoting dignity and health.
